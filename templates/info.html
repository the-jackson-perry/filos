According to the Mayo Clinic and countless studies, socializing with friends, family, and strangers is a foundational aspect of maintaining good mental health. With the onset of COVID, socializing has become an increasingly challenging task. This is especially difficult for people who have lost loved ones, have gone through a breakup, or have been alienated from their family. In order to combat this, we have built a platform for isolated people to reach out to others in simmilar situations. Below we have some charts and graphs visualizing some relevant data.
